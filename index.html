Intro

I bring a unique blend of analytical rigor, stakeholder engagement, and service excellence to deliver measurable impact across financial services and customer-centric environments.

My technical toolkit includes Excel, SQL, Python, Power Apps and Power BI, while my collaborative nature and Agile experience enable me to translate business needs into impactful solutions.

By the way, check out my data and fintech projects.

Covid-19 Dashboard

This is a project about the infection and mortality trends of COVID-19 based on data from the World Health Organisation.

The project was built using PowerBI with the aim to visualise the trends and also provide more information on how the pandemic affected the world. This reduced manual reporting work by 10+ hours weekly by automating dynamic report updates.

Online Shopping Prediction

This is a project designed to predict shopping intention amongst buyers who visit an Online e-commerce webiste.

The project evaluated 14000+ customer interaction data using Python and created a model which predicts purchase behaviour and predict the likelihood of repeat purchases. This tool can be useful in improving marketing to target high-propensity buyers and improve sales forecast.

Athlete Monitoring System

This is a project designed to review relationship between the physical profiles of football players and their on-field performance. It also aims to predict injury risk based on their performance.

The project assessed data from player profiles at an English Championship Football Club to identify risk factors and training performance patterns. I engineered a predictive algorithm with 95% accuracy using Python for injury prediction based on historical performance data

I-Mart

This is a FinTech prototype designed for secure gadget ownership authentication and resale. The aim of this product is to reduce fraud risks in secondary sale markets.

Oversaw the development and led a team of 5 techincal experts to define product requirements and ensure seamless implementation.

PLEND

This is a FinTech prototype designed for unbanked and financially disadvantaged individuals. The product uses crowdfunding and informal credit frameworks to provide the financially excluded with access to credit-building solutions.

I led the project and handled the creation of user stories, mapping of customer journeys and managed the prioritisation of features for iterative development.
